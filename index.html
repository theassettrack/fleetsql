Frequently Asked Questions

How long can I store data with FleetSQL?

Forever. Unlike Wialon, we don't delete historical data. Keep years of fleet data for trend analysis, compliance, and analytics.

What SQL access do I get?

Full PostgreSQL access with your own credentials. Query any data, join tables, create views, build custom aggregations. Connect via psql, DBeaver, DataGrip, or any PostgreSQL client. Complete control.

How much will I actually save?

Wialon storage extensions cost $0.50 per 400-day extension. Storing 5 years = $2/unit/month. FleetSQL with annual billing is $0.30/unit/month for unlimited storage plus SQL access. For a 200-unit fleet, that saves $340/month versus Wialon storage alone.

What visualization tools can I use?

Any PostgreSQL tool. Popular options: Grafana, Metabase, Power BI, Tableau, Looker Studio, or custom dashboards. We help configure your tool of choice.

Do I own the data?

Yes, 100%. It's in your dedicated PostgreSQL database with your credentials. Export, back up, migrate anywhere. Complete ownership. If you cancel, keep all your data.

How is data separated for multiple clients?

Each client has their own isolated database with separate credentials and Wialon tokens. Complete separation. Client A never sees Client B's data. You control access independently per database.
